Privacy Policy

Welcome to RiseTechGlobal ("we," "our," or "us"), a provider of cloud computing solutions, hardware products, and network infrastructure services. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you interact with our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company details, and job title when you register, request a demo, or contact our sales/support teams.
  • Business Information: Company size, industry, and IT infrastructure details for service customization.
  • Payment Information: Billing details for purchases/subscriptions (processed securely via third-party providers; we don't store payment data).
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, browser type, and usage patterns for analytics.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for session management, preferences, and analytics (manage via browser settings).
  • Support Data: Information provided when seeking technical assistance for our products/services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and optimize our cloud services, hardware solutions, and network products.
  • To process orders, manage subscriptions, and provide invoices.
  • To offer technical support and troubleshoot issues.
  • To improve product functionality and develop new features.
  • To send service announcements, security updates, and (with consent) marketing communications.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements.
  • To conduct market research and business analytics.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Cloud Service Partners: For infrastructure provisioning and maintenance.
  • Hardware Vendors: For order fulfillment and warranty services.
  • Payment Processors: For secure transaction handling.
  • IT Service Providers: For website hosting, security, and analytics.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales.

4. Data Security Measures

  • Enterprise-grade encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Role-based access controls to sensitive information
  • Secure data center facilities for physical protection
  • Employee privacy training and confidentiality agreements

While we implement robust security measures, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Data Rights

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw marketing consent (use unsubscribe links or contact us)
  • Request data portability where applicable

6. International Data Transfers

As a global provider, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border transfers.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may integrate with or link to:

  • Cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
  • Payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics)
  • CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot)

These services have their own privacy policies governing your data.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data:

  • For active customers: Throughout service period + 7 years for tax/legal compliance
  • For prospects: Until consent is withdrawn or 2 years of inactivity
  • For website visitors: As needed for analytics (typically 26 months)

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are business-oriented and not intended for individuals under 16. We don't knowingly collect children's data.

10. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. Significant changes will be notified via email or website notice.
